A BUSINESS BUILT
ON PARTNERSHIPS

WE DON'T HAVE WHOLESALE ACCOUNTS WE HAVE PARTNERS.

Because we believe true partnership is what leads to success for all.


We pride ourselves on providing quality specialty coffee to espresso bars and cafes, but above all else it's the partnerships we make with businesses that we truly value. We want to partner with people who believe what we believe – that coffee has the power to do good.


Committing to a partnership is not something we take lightly, so we only partner with the right people. We look for partners with the same long-term focus and an uncompromising commitment to quality as we have – that is a non-negotiable for us.


We are proud of our consistency; this is why all our partners use the same espresso equipment as we do in our flagship cafes. We tenaciously defend quality, which is why we work closely with our partners and have regular assessments to ensure the same high quality standard is met each and every time.


Our partnerships go beyond the supply of quality coffee. We understand that our partner's success is our success, and that flows on to the success of our producing partners. So for us it's about helping our cafe partners with everything from in-depth barista training to business planning and expansion when the time comes. We expect a high standard, but don't shy away from helping achieve this standard. We believe partnership does really mean we have a shared responsibility to delivering amazing quality coffee that lives up to the Campos brand every time.
